About Bharat Pharma Chem

Built on research, sourcing strength and trusted leadership

Bharat Pharma Chem works across advanced intermediates, specialty chemicals and Pharma Chem ingredients, serving pharmaceuticals, APIs, agrochemicals, dyes, intermediates, fragrances and bulk chemical markets.

Global chemical supply with focused technical capability

The company combines market knowledge, formulation support and sourcing partnerships to deliver dependable chemical solutions across India and international markets.

Our Company

A wholesaler, distributor and trader of quality bulk intermediates and fine chemicals.

Our business model includes specializations in advanced intermediates, specialty chemicals and Pharma Chem ingredients. By working on development and formulation of new chemical products with innovative concepts, Bharat Pharma Chem has carved a niche in its segment of operational activities.

The company has earned appreciation as a wholesaler, distributor and trader of a wide assortment of high quality bulk intermediates and fine chemicals throughout the global market.

Our products are used in a wide range of industries such as pharmaceuticals and APIs, agrochemicals, dyes and intermediates, perfumery and fragrance, and bulk intermediates. Today, Bharat Pharma Chem supplies its range across India and continues to expand through reliable sourcing and customer-focused support.
